Who am I?

Hello Viewers! I am Cassidy Koedam and I am a graphic design student at Grand View Univesity. I spend my days in Des Moines Iowa lifting weights, loving on any kitty I see, making art, and playing video games.

What I Do For Fun

I love working out, specifically lifting weights. I can squat 165 lbs, and bench 135. I love seeing the fat come off (when I'm behaving diet-wise) and the muscle slowly pack on. It's a huge transformative process and a huge stress reliever for me. I also have a nerve disorder and without the gym, I would be a pill pushing, accupuncture getting, miserable excuse for a human being. Weight lifting has been such a blessing for my health and my life would not exist had I not found it. Anyways, I love to paint, draw, and do photography as well. That's all pretty self explanatory. Video games are a fun socializing thing for me, most of my buddies live in Chicago and talking to them is a highlight of my day. Along with my two black cats, who wear bow ties and are total instagram models. I'll insert some fun fan art below.
